# WISDOM: Build the Life You Want (Brooks & Winfrey)

> *Happiness is a direction, not a destination. Acceptance of both positive and negative emotions is required for a fulfilling life.*

## 1. The Three Macronutrients
*   **Enjoyment:** (Pleasure + Communion + Consciousness). Pleasure is animalistic (Limbic); Enjoyment is human (Prefrontal Cortex). *Fix: Add people and memory to pleasure.*
*   **Satisfaction:** (Earned Success). Due to hedonic adaptation, goals reset baseline. *Fix: Manage the denominator (Satisfaction = Have / Want). Intentionally want less.*
*   **Meaning:** (Coherence + Significance). Forged through hardship and suffering. It provides the "Why" that connects life together.

## 2. Metacognition (The Engine)
*   **The Gap:** Thinking about your thinking. Shifting processing from the reactive **Limbic System** to the executive **Prefrontal Cortex**.
*   **Strategy:** Observe emotions as a third party. Journaling is the fastest way to trigger this shift and reduce the intensity of negative feelings.

## 3. The PANAS Affect Profiles
Identify your nature to manage blind spots:
*   **Mad Scientist:** High Positive / High Negative (Extreme cycles).
*   **The Judge:** Low Positive / Low Negative (Steady, but can lack joy).
*   **The Cheerleader:** High Positive / Low Negative (Optimistic, but can ignore threats).
*   **The Poet:** Low Positive / High Negative (Melancholic, creative, empathetic).

## 4. The Four Pillars of Stability
1.  **Faith/Philosophy:** Connection to something transcendent; shrinking the ego.
2.  **Family:** Managing expectations and avoiding emotional contagion.
3.  **Friendship:** Pruning "Deal" friends (utility) to make space for "Real" friends (virtue).
4.  **Work:** Derived from **Earned Success** (creating value) and **Serving Others**.
